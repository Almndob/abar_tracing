# Product Overview

## Abar Tracing System

Abar Tracing is an internal truck tracking and fleet management system developed for Abar Company. It provides real-time GPS monitoring of company vehicles and shipment logistics across the Kingdom.

### Core Purpose

- **Internal Use Only**: Not a public service - designed exclusively for Abar Company's internal operations
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Monitor truck locations, shipment status, and delivery operations in real-time
- **Fleet Management**: Track active trucks, completed deliveries, and identify late shipments

### Key Features

1. **Public Tracking Interface**
   - Customers can track shipments using tracking IDs
   - Real-time GPS location display on interactive maps
   - Shipment status updates and delivery ETAs

2. **Admin Dashboard**
   - Employee authentication and secure access
   - Fleet overview with active trucks and delivery statistics
   - Shipment management (status updates, manual adjustments)
   - Truck management (driver assignments, status tracking)
   - Report generation and performance analytics

3. **Core Capabilities**
   - GPS-based real-time location tracking
   - Automated delivery ETA calculations
   - Route optimization and fuel consumption monitoring
   - Driver behavior monitoring and compliance tracking
   - Instant alerts for delays or route changes

### Target Users

- **Administrators**: Company employees managing fleet operations
- **Drivers**: Truck drivers with assigned vehicles
- **Stakeholders**: Internal departments tracking shipments

### Business Context

The system operates within Hail region (Saudi Arabia) with location data centered around Hail city coordinates (27.5250°N, 41.6900°E).
